Being a list of programming, database, writing, multimedia, video, games, social media, mobile groups in Roanoke, Virginia. Or within striking distance of Roanoke. To submit your group or event contact Joe Crawford on twitter [@artlung](http://twitter.com/?status=%40artlung%20RE%20Roanoke%20tech:) or email <joe@artlung.com>.
